Awards

ACNS offers two types of awards:

1. Student Travel Awards

Up to 10 student awards will be available to assist with travel costs for students ($250/award).

To be eligible to apply for an ACNS Travel Award, you must:

  • Be a current student (PhD, Masters, Honours, undergraduate)
  • Be the presenting (first) author on a submitted abstract
  • Be a current ACNS student member
  • Complete student registration for ACNS2016
  • Both full-time and part-time students can apply
  • Submit your abstract and accompanying information by September 15

When submitting your abstract, you will be asked to:

  • Indicate your intention to apply for an ACNS Travel Award
  • Attach a single pdf document consisting of:
    • A 2-page, single-spaced CV including
      • a brief track record statement (max 200 words),
      • a list of any research papers and previous conference presentations,
      • a statement of any significant career disruptions (if applicable).
    • A 1-page signed statement from your supervisor supporting your application and confirming your student status (including your institution and student number).

Travel awards recipients will be notified early in October.

2. Presentation awards

Junior post-doctoral researchers and students are also eligible for oral and poster presentation awards.

To be eligible for a presentation award, post-docs and students must be:

  • A current student (PhD, Masters, Honours, undergraduate) or have graduated with a PhD in the last 12 months
  • The presenting (first) author
  • A current ACNS student member
  • Registered for ACNS2016

Presentation awards will be presented at the closing of the conference before the AGM.
