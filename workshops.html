Workshops

Early Career Researcher (ECR) Workshop

Thursday 24th November 9:00 am - noon

This year's ECR workshop will focus on writing successful grant applications. It will consist of presentation sessions by successful applicants and selection panel members, followed by a Q&A panel with extensive opportunity for interaction.

More information about schedule and speakers to come.

This event will be followed by social events organised by the ECR Committee.

Mid-Career Researcher (MCR) Workshop

Thursday 24th November 1:00 – 4:00 pm

We are introducing an MCR workshop that will focus on pathways beyond ECR status. This workshop is targeted towards the transition from senior post-doc to junior faculty, and balancing research, teaching, admin, family etc. The workshop will be moderated by Em.Professor Pat Michie. It will include brief presentations by researchers at various levels of seniority followed by extensive opportunity for Q&A.

More information about schedule and speakers to come.
