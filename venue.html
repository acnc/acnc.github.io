Venue Information

The 6th Australasian Cognitive Neuroscience Society Conference is hosted by the University of Newcastle and will be held at the Ramada Resort, Shoal Bay. Located on the beachfront at Shoal Bay, the Ramada is located central to the Shoal Bay village, a part of the larger Port Stephens and Hunter Valley areas.

The Ramada Resort is a family friendly beachfront resort, a radical but inviting contrast to the usual inner city conference venues.
