Accomodation

ACNS2016 is hosted at the Whitesands conference centre at the Ramada Resort Shoal Bay.

The Ramada is located just across the sandy Shoal Bay beachfront. It features a range of studio, 1-bd, 2-bd and 3-bd apartments and facilities to suit all traveller types. Rooms offer spacious layouts, air-conditioning and feature comfortable seating and dining areas. In-room amenities include satellite TV, DVD player and either full kitchens or kitchenettes. 

The resort offers a range of dining options, access to a lagoon style outdoor pool with children’s wading area as well as an indoor 25m lap-pool, multiple spas, fitness centre, bike hire facilities and complimentary parking.

The resort is located in the heart of Shoal Bay just next to local shops, restaurants and a small supermarket, allowing you to have everything you need at your fingertips.
